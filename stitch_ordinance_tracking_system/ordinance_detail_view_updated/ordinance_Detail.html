arrow_drop_down

Zoning Amendment: Commercial Core District Overlay

An ordinance to amend Title 17 (Zoning) of the Municipal Code to establish a new overlay district intended to promote mixed-use development and pedestrian-friendly infrastructure in the central commercial corridor.

WHEREAS, the City Council has determined that the current zoning regulations in the central commercial district do not adequately incentivize the type of high-density, mixed-use development envisioned in the Comprehensive Plan; and

WHEREAS, the establishment of a Commercial Core District Overlay will provide a regulatory framework to guide future development, ensure architectural consistency, and enhance the public realm; and

WHEREAS, the Planning Commission held a public hearing on March 15, 2024, and voted to recommend approval of this amendment to the City Council.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE MUNICIPALITY AS FOLLOWS:

Section 1. Amendment of Zoning Map. The official Zoning Map is hereby amended to apply the Commercial Core District Overlay (CCDO) to the parcels designated on the map attached hereto as Exhibit A.

Section 2. Permitted Uses. Within the CCDO, ground-floor commercial uses shall be required for all new construction facing primary arterial streets. Residential uses shall be permitted on upper floors without density restrictions, subject to design review.

Section 3. Development Standards. Building heights within the CCDO may be increased to a maximum of 85 feet, provided that the development incorporates public amenities such as plazas, widened sidewalks, or public art, as detailed in the Commercial Core Design Guidelines.
